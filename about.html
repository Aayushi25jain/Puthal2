About Puthal

Transforming Mental Health Care Through Innovation

Our Mission

At Puthal, we believe that mental health should be accessible, personalized, and effective for everyone. Our mission is to break down barriers to mental health care by leveraging cutting-edge technology to create solutions that truly understand and respond to individual needs.

We're committed to creating a world where seeking help for mental health is as natural and straightforward as taking care of your physical health.
