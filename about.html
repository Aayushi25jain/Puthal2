About Puthal

Puthal is dedicated to reimagining mental health and well-being for everyone. Our mission is to empower individuals to prioritize their mental health through innovative technology, AI-driven mood tracking, mindfulness exercises, and a supportive community. We believe in making mental wellness accessible, engaging, and stigma-free.

Our Vision

To create a world where mental health is valued as much as physical health, and everyone has the tools and support they need to thrive.
