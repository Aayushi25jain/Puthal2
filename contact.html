Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I expect a response?

We typically respond to general inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For technical support, our average response time is 4-6 hours. Crisis support inquiries are prioritized and handled immediately.

Is my information secure when I contact you?

Yes, all communications through our contact forms are encrypted and handled according to HIPAA privacy standards. We never share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent.

Can I schedule a demo of the platform?

Absolutely! Select "Partnership Opportunity" or "General Inquiry" from the subject dropdown and mention your interest in a demo. Our team will coordinate a personalized walkthrough at your convenience.

Do you offer support in languages other than English?

Currently, our primary support is in English, but we have Spanish and Mandarin support available. Please mention your preferred language in your message.

What if I'm having a mental health crisis?

If you're experiencing a crisis, please don't wait for our response. Contact emergency services (911) or the crisis resources listed on this page immediately. Our platform is not a substitute for emergency mental health care.

Can I suggest new features for the platform?

Yes! We love hearing from our users. Select "Feedback & Suggestions" from the subject dropdown and share your ideas. We regularly review user suggestions for future updates.
